About InsightHire

InsightHire provides AI-powered candidate assessment, analyzing facial expressions and body language to assist in better hiring decisions. Our platform helps you understand candidates beyond their resume and prepared answers, giving you deeper insights into their potential fit for your organization.

Key Benefits

  • Real-time AI assessment - Get instant feedback on candidate responses and non-verbal cues
  • Smart question generation - Let our AI suggest questions based on candidate profiles and responses
  • Comprehensive behavioral insights - Access detailed analytics about candidate behavior patterns
  • Seamless integration - Works with popular video conferencing platforms like Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet
  • Time-saving tools - Automated note-taking and summarization of interview highlights

Getting Started

Begin using InsightHire in three simple steps:

  1. Create your interviewer account
  2. Set up your interview preferences and assessment criteria
  3. Schedule interviews through our integrated calendar system

Our platform will guide you through the process and provide training resources to maximize your interview effectiveness.
