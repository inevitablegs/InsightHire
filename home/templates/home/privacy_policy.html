Information We Collect

At InsightHire, we collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our services. This may include:

- Contact information (name, email, phone number)

- Professional information (resume, work history, skills)

- Account credentials

We also automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

- Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

Your data security is our priority. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
